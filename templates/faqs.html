Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How does EduPathway work?

EduPathway analyzes your academic scores and personal interests to recommend the most suitable study streams.

❓ Is the recommendation system AI-based?

Yes, EduPathway uses a smart combination of rule-based logic and machine learning techniques to suggest optimal paths.

❓ What details do I need to provide?

You need to enter your academic scores and a few preferences or interests related to subjects or career goals.

❓ Is my data stored anywhere?

No, we do not store or share your personal data. Your privacy and confidentiality are our top priorities.

❓ Can I get career advice too?

Yes, apart from stream recommendations, you can explore blog posts and tips on career development.

❓ Who can use EduPathway?

EduPathway is ideal for students in high school, parents, and educators looking for insightful academic direction.

❓ Is EduPathway free to use?

Absolutely! Our platform is completely free to access and use for all students and schools.
