🎮 Path to Becoming a Game Developer

📓 Subjects in 11th and 12th:

  • 🧮 Mathematics
  • 💻 Computer Science / Information Practices
  • 🔭 Physics
  • ✍️ English

🎓 Entrance Exams & Degrees:

  • 📝 JEE Main (for Engineering Colleges)
  • 👨‍💻 B.Tech in Computer Science with Game Development Specialization
  • 🎨 B.Sc. in Animation & Game Design
  • 🕹️ Bachelor's in Game Design or Interactive Media

🏫 Top Institutes Offering Game Dev Programs:

  • 🎓 VIT University, Vellore
  • 🎨 MIT Institute of Design, Pune
  • 🌄 UPES, Dehradun
  • 🖌️ National Institute of Design (NID)

💡 Did You Know?

The Indian gaming industry is expected to grow to $8.6 billion by 2027! Careers in game development, design, and animation are now highly sought-after.
