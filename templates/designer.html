🎨 Designer Pathway

📚 Subjects to Study (11th & 12th):

  • 🖌️ Fine Arts
  • 🧮 Mathematics (optional but helpful)
  • ✍️ English
  • 💻 Computer Science (useful for graphic/UI design)

📝 Entrance Exams & Degrees:

  • 🧠 NID DAT (National Institute of Design - Design Aptitude Test)
  • 🖍️ UCEED (Undergraduate Common Entrance Examination for Design)
  • 📐 CEED (Common Entrance Examination for Design - Postgraduate)
  • 🎓 B.Des (Bachelor of Design)
  • 🎓 M.Des (Master of Design)

🏫 Top Institutes:

  • 🎓 National Institute of Design (NID), Ahmedabad
  • 🏙️ Industrial Design Centre (IDC), IIT Bombay
  • 👗 National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT)
  • 🎨 Srishti Institute of Art, Design and Technology, Bangalore
  • 📍 MIT Institute of Design, Pune

💡 Did You Know?

From UI/UX to fashion, India’s design industry is expanding at a rate of 23-25% annually. There is a growing demand for visual storytellers and design strategists.
